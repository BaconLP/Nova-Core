Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Nova Core collects the following types of information:

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Server IDs and channel IDs for bot configuration
  • User IDs for moderation logs and level tracking
  • Message metadata (not content) for statistics
  • Command usage data for improvement purposes

User-Provided Information:

  • Configuration settings and preferences
  • Custom commands and automation rules
  • Moderation actions and reasons

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing bot functionality and features
  • Maintaining server configurations and settings
  • Moderation logging and audit trails
  • Improving bot performance and features
  • Generating anonymized usage statistics

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • Anonymized data for research purposes

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular backups and data integrity checks

5. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide services:

  • Configuration data: Until bot is removed from server
  • Moderation logs: Up to 90 days or as configured
  • User statistics: Anonymized and indefinite
  • Error logs: Up to 30 days

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we store
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection where possible
  • Correct inaccurate information

7. Third-Party Services

Nova Core may integrate with third-party services:

  • Discord API for bot functionality
  • Music streaming services for audio features
  • Analytics services for performance monitoring

8. Children's Privacy

Nova Core is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced in our support server.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us through our Discord support server or email privacy@novacore.bot
